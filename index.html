Pseudocode for Adventure Game

Concept

(Quest Route)

  • You ((choose)Possibly add after MVP has been done.) a adventurer to take on the quest. You wake up in a village, and choose what to do. You are already well known for helping around the village. (You have a couple choices while in the village.) You take a quest. As you walk to the location, you grow tired. While tired, you take a nap.
  • You wake up in a dim room. Stone walls cover around you with one lit torch.You're vision starts to get better again. As you look around you see a wagon, and slowly squint your eyes. See a man under this wagon. Man stares back and sees you. (Decsion making comes here and have a conversation).
  • You get out of the room come in a room with plenty of boxes around the edges of the room. Choices to pick to look in boxes or not.
  • Next room you hear chants and see people with robes around them.

(Quest Route)

  • You fight these people with robes on. there are 2-3 of them. (Interface will change to a fighting HUD). (After this, You can choose to loot them or go back to the previous room.)
  • After beating them, switches back to text Interface, and see another door. The room with the fight happening has fireplace in the back wall, a big round table in the middle with candles, bottles, books, and red table cloth.
  • If you choose to walk through the wooden door at the side of the wall, You will see a Ragged robe on this person with his hood over his head. In his hand, he has a green skull with flames coming out from the top.
  • He will give you a choice to join him or fight him.
  • After sometime defeating him and escaping. The man you helped gives you a treasure!(considering you saved him)
